Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the need of online or digital marketing?

Staying synchronised with the way people consume information and content these days, businesses have no better option than a strong online presence if they have to find, attract and retain customers. This is why one cannot ignore online marketing in this time and age.

What is the difference between SEO and SEM?

SEO is search engine optimisation. It uses various tactics and actions to land a website or a webpage higher up in Search Engine Result Pages or SERPs. SEO is all about organic search and its clicks and views always go unpaid. SEM is search engine marketing. Unlike SEO it mainly focuses on paid results. In other words the results do appear but are paid for if and when a user interacts with the results. Both SEO and SEM are equally crucial to achieve the best results.

How much is the cost of digital marketing campaigns?

Just like conventional marketing, digital marketing cost varies dramatically. But unlike conventional marketing it offers some scope to enjoy the service for free. For example, SEO is mostly free. It requires only your time and effort. Even starting a page on social media and posting there is mostly free or has a pretty low cost. On the other hand paid advertising like PPC is definitely expensive. You may also have to invest money in content creation, e-Mail automation software, CRM and such other things.
